Colle System

In my opinion it is the best way to play with white when you are starting to play chess, it is a very easy system to use in any situation, always remember to control your jump to: E4.

Italian Opening

This opening is already a bit more advanced, but what I like is that aggressive play attacking the point:F7 with your bishop and then attacking with the jump of the knight eating his castle.

Queen's Gambit

For what I like this opening is because of its high control of the center, its great strategies and its great variations when playing it make it interesting, and yes obviously because of the ofc netflix series.

Dutch Defense

The interesting thing about this defense is the easy playability and that it can be played against various game starts and rhythms, my favorite is the aggressive attacking as soon as possible the checkmate in short castling.

Sicilian Defense

Being the most popular against:E4, the good thing about the Sicilian is its solid game positioning, its easy use in the game, its high combat and its drastic changes to other variations.

Scandinavian Defense

Being one of the oldest is another way to counter against :E4, what I like about this defense is its early game attacking the center, its variety of continuation options make it an interesting way to play.
